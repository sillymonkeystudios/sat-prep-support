Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my progress?

Currently, progress cannot be reset from within the app. If you need to start fresh, please contact support and we'll be happy to help.

How do I report a bug or issue?

Please email us at the address above with details about the issue you're experiencing, including:

  • What you were doing when the issue occurred
  • What you expected to happen
  • What actually happened
  • Your device type and OS version (if relevant)

How do I sync my data across devices?

Your progress is automatically synced when you sign in with your account. Make sure you're using the same account on all devices.

How do I join or create a leaderboard?

Navigate to the Leaderboard section in the app. You can view global leaderboards or create your own private leaderboard and invite friends!

How are my SAT scores calculated?

Your SAT scores are calculated based on your performance in practice questions, using the same scoring methodology as the actual SAT exam.

About SatUP!

SatUP! is a gamified SAT preparation app that makes studying feel like playing a game. Practice daily, track your progress, compete on leaderboards, and achieve your SAT goals!

Features include:

  • Daily challenges to maintain your streak
  • XP and achievement system
  • Progress tracking with detailed analytics
  • Global and private leaderboards
  • SAT score history and predictions
